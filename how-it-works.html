How It Works

Downloading videos with VidGrab Pro is simple and fast. Follow these four easy steps:

Step 1: Copy Video URL

Go to your favorite video platform (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, etc.) and copy the video URL from your browser's address bar.

Step 2: Paste URL

Return to VidGrab Pro and paste the copied URL into the input field on our homepage. You can use the paste button or simply press Ctrl+V.

Step 3: Choose Quality

Select your preferred video quality from available options: 480p (SD), 720p (HD), 1080p (Full HD), 2K, or 4K when available.

Step 4: Download

Click the download button and your video will start downloading immediately to your device. That's it!
