Frequently Asked Questions

Is VidGrab Pro free to use?
Yes, our basic video downloading service is completely free. We offer premium features for power users, but the core functionality remains free forever.
What video qualities are available?
We support multiple quality options including 480p (SD), 720p (HD), 1080p (Full HD), 2K, and 4K when available from the source platform.
Is it legal to download videos?
VidGrab Pro is a tool for personal use. Please respect copyright laws and only download videos that you have permission to use or that are in the public domain.
Do you support mobile devices?
Yes, our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.
Why is my download not working?
This could be due to several reasons: unsupported platform, video privacy settings, or temporary server issues. Try again later or contact support.
Can I download entire playlists?
Currently, we support individual video downloads. Playlist downloading is a feature we're developing for future updates.
How long does it take to download a video?
Download speed depends on your internet connection and video size. Most videos download in under a minute with a standard broadband connection.
Do you store any of my data?
We respect your privacy. We don't store downloaded videos or personal information. Video URLs are processed in real-time and not saved.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is required for basic downloads. However, creating a free account gives you access to download history and faster speeds.
What formats are supported?
We support MP4, WEBM, MKV, and AVI formats. MP4 is recommended for best compatibility.
